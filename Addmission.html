Admissions Process

  1. Complete the online application form by 24th August, 2023.
  2. Submit your academic transcripts, standardized test scores, letters of recommendation, and other required documents.
  3. Craft a compelling personal statement that highlights your passions, achievements, and aspirations.

Upon acceptance, submit the required enrollment deposit by 24th August, 2023 to secure your spot.
